About

First impressions take mere seconds and the critical decisions on choice of a product and services are usually determined by outstanding design. Time and time again, only the best design connects users intuitively and effectively to the advanced technologies that otherwise are complex and difficult to understand and use.

The opportunities I have had are all transcending the connection between technology, human interaction & consumption, and application of concepts through UX design - with emphasis on trust, authentication, discovery and synthesis of user requirements and completely addressing user needs via physical and digital interfaces.

The root of my motivations have stemmed from my own drive to develop long lasting relationships with my team and purposeful connections with personas from all walks of life. By emphasizing the importance of UX design, we can reach out to the right audiences like never before.

When I'm not Designing...

When I am not designing multimedia interfaces I am using other forms of unconventional communication and expression. After graduating from Pacific Northwest Ballet School in 2017, I continue my dance training at Harvard Ballet Company, where I witness how creation and choreography can tell other fascinating narratives when words are not enough. It further inspires me to forge novel and creative pathways that can be applied in digital spaces, from the ground up.
