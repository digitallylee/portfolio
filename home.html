The Move is an organization at MIT's Department of Urban Studies and Planning (DUSP); rebuilding the public's muscle memory for democracy.

In 2020, The Move released the Civic Design Framework (CDF) white paper. A tool for building engagement processes that can move a divided nation to more just outcomes.

The CDF is intended for municipalities, community organizations, and public-facing institutions

I was the lead designer that helped create the interactive tool.

The tool was made to help users physically and visually interact with higher-level concepts. The spinner helps readers digest the material as an more approchable and relatable fun aid to their learning.

In collaboration with other undergrads, current MDs, postgrads, and Professor Ceasar McDowell.
