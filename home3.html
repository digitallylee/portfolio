For CS 220, Human Computer Interaction at Wellesley College

A redesign of the current Wellesley food portal.

The main problems users face is having to navigate through the app easily and quickly because the layout makes it hard to compare different dining halls. Our app creates a streamlined process for these Wellesley students to quickly compare dining halls and find a place to eat.

I was responsible for creating all wireframes and prototypes. I also aided in user research, interviews and the implementation of feedback. All in collaboration with Wellesley undergrads.
